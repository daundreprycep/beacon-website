About

Navigating around campus doesn’t have to be an intimidating experience. As a brand new student, it is Beacon’s role to get you around campus with ease.

How it works

Enter where you would like to go

You are able to go where you want, when you want on campus with ease. It could be a specific classroom, wing, restaurant, school services etc. Just input where and Beacon will show you the way!

View your destination in AR!

To help you navigate campus, the path to your destination will be visible to you in Augmented Reality

Scan key objects

Ever wondered what that statue on campus was and why it’s there? Now you can find out! Just point your phone at the object and Beacon will display a little biography that you can learn from.

Available for your smartphone!

Experience a new way to navigate campus with Beacon. The best part is that it's 100% free! Available for iOS and Android.
