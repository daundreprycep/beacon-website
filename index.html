About HER

Navigating around campus doesn’t have to be an intimidating experience. As a brand new student, it is Beacon’s role to get you around campus with ease.

How it works

Enter where you want to go

You’re able to go where you want, when you want. It could be a specific classroom, restaurant, or school service. Just input where and the app will show you the way!

View your destination in AR!

To help you navigate around campus, the path to your destination will be displayed to you in Augmented Reality. Just follow the arrow and you’ll be there in no time!

Scan points of interests

With your phone, you can find information about a certain point of interest! Just point your phone at the object and it will display a little bio that you can learn from.
